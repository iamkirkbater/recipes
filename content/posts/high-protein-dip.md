A high-protein dip that's perfect with chips or sliced veggies.

## Ingredients

- 50 grams of protein
- Quest chips or any chip or sliced veggie


## Instructions

1. Prepare the dip with the 50 grams of protein.
2. Add your choice of Quest chips, any chip, or sliced veggies for dipping.


## Notes

This recipe is from the flavor-FULL cookbook and it's great for making healthy choices without compromising taste.
