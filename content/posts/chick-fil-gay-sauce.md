## Ingredients

- 20g Honey
- 70g Light Mayo
- 20g Mustard
- 15g (2tbsp) Sugar Free BBQ Sauce ( a smokey hickory flavor for best result)
- 1 tsp Apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp Onion Powder
- 5-10g Low-Calorie Sweetener


## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients and let sit for at least 20m before using for flavors to meld.
