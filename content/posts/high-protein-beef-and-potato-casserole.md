A high protein meal to support gym-goers, featuring beef, bacon, potatoes, and a homemade sauce.

{{< recipedetails PrepTime="15 minutes" TotalTime="1 hour" Servings="8" >}}

## Ingredients

- 3 lbs 96/4 beef
- 12 Sargento ultra-thin slices of cheddar
- 48 oz petite gold potatoes
- 10 slices beef bacon
- 16 oz onion
- 200g 0% Greek yogurt
- 80g mustard
- 80g light mayo
- 50g no sugar ketchup
- 50g relish
- Garlic powder


## Instructions

1. Start off with 48 ounces of petite gold potatoes. Spray with avocado spray lightly and cover with everything seasoning. Shake to coat. Place in the oven at 425°F for 45 minutes.
2. Cook 3 pounds lean beef with 16 ounces of onions, seasoning heavily with burger seasoning. Break it up as it cooks.
3. Cook 10 slices of beef bacon with a lid to prevent grease splatter.
4. Mix plain Greek yogurt, light mayo, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, no sugar ketchup, sweet relish, and garlic powder to make the sauce.
5. After cooking, place cooked beef over potatoes, then top with bacon, and finally lay 12 ultra-thin cheese slices over it. Return to the oven for 5 minutes to melt the cheese.


## Notes

**Tip:** If beef bacon is unavailable, you can substitute with turkey bacon.
