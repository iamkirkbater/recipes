## Ingredients

- 3lbs 96/4 beef
- 300g onions (~2 medium onions)
- 1 pack chili seasoning
- 8 oz protein bone broth
- 3 cans kidney beans
- 1 can rotel no salt
- 1 can rotel with salt (mild)
- 100g plain Greek yogurt
- 1 kraft fat free cheddar cheese
- 16 servings (35cal) tortilla strips


## Instructions

1. Cook 3lbs beef, Add chili package, Add 300g white onions, Mix & let cook 
2. When it’s half way done add 3 cans DRAINED kidney beans, 1 can rotel no salt, 1 can rotel with salt. Mix and let this good until the meet is cooked 
3. When cooked add 100g plain Greek yogurt mix and place into 8 meal prep containers and add 1 servings fat free cheese into each container 
4. Then take tortilla strips and weigh out 8 zip lock bags of 14 grams so your chips don’t go bad.
