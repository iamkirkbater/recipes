Flavorful Middle Eastern & Mediterranean style beef kabobs made on a sheet pan for a simple and delicious meal.

## Ingredients

- 2 lb ground beef
- 1 small grated or processed white onion
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 finely minced serrano pepper
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1/2 cup chopped parsley
- a couple pinches salt
- 1.5 tsp cumin
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tbsp Aleppo pepper
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp oregano
- 2 sliced Roma tomatoes
- 1/2 thinly sliced red onion
- 1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tbsp sumac
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- salt & pepper
- 1/3 cup tahini
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 grated garlic clove
- 1/4 cup cold water
- salt to taste


## Instructions

1. Mix 2 lb ground beef, 1 small grated or processed white onion, 3 garlic cloves, 1 finely minced serrano pepper, 2 tbsp tomato paste, 1/2 cup chopped parsley, a couple pinches salt, 1.5 tsp cumin, 1 tbsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tbsp Aleppo pepper, 1/4 tsp cinnamon, and 1 tsp oregano. Let sit for 30-90 minutes.
2. Spray a sheet pan with avocado oil. Press the meat mixture evenly across the whole pan until it’s about 1/2 to 3/4 inch thick.
3. Using a sharp knife, cut long vertical lines through the meat mixture almost to the bottom of the pan (don’t slice all the way through). Run the side of your knife or a spatula along each edge of the cuts to smooth and round them out, so each strip looks like an individual kabob.
4. Bake for 20-22 minutes at 425°F.
5. Remove from the oven, spoon out the excess liquid into a small bowl, mix with 1 tbsp tomato paste, and brush it over the kabobs. Return to the oven to broil for 2.5-3 minutes. This step intensifies the flavor and gives you that grilled look and taste.
6. Let rest a few minutes before cutting along your score lines.
7. Whisk together 1/3 cup tahini, juice of 1 lemon, 1 grated garlic clove, 1/4 cup cold water, and salt to taste. Slowly add the water until it’s smooth, creamy, and pourable. Taste and adjust lemon or salt as needed.
8. Toss 2 sliced Roma tomatoes, 1/2 thinly sliced red onion, 1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley, 1 tbsp sumac, juice of 1/2 lemon, salt & pepper.
9. Warm lavash or pita, spread the tahini sauce, top with kabob, salad, fresh parsley & mint, and garnish with sumac.
