A low-carb dish featuring chicken, broccoli, jalapeños, bacon, and cheese.

## Ingredients

- Rotisserie chicken
- Broccoli
- Cheddar cheese
- Cream cheese
- Splash of almond milk (to thin out cheese mixture)
- Jalapeños
- Crispy bacon


## Instructions

1. Start with broccoli and add shredded chicken breast or your protein of choice.
2. Mix sharp cheddar cheese and room temperature cream cheese.
3. Pour the cheese mixture over the chicken and broccoli and spread it evenly.
4. Add diced jalapeños on top.
5. Finish with crispy bacon on top.
6. Top with a little more cheese to get a nice crisp.
7. Bake at 375°F for about 15 minutes.
