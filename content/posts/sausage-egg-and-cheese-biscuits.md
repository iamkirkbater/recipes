A cost-effective alternative to fast food breakfast sandwiches, these homemade sausage, egg, and cheese biscuits are perfect for meal prep.

## Ingredients

- Sausage
- Eggs
- Cheese
- Biscuits


## Instructions

1. Prepare sausage, eggs, cheese, and biscuits to create sandwiches.
2. Wrap each sandwich individually for storage.
3. Store half of the sandwiches in the fridge and the other half in the freezer for long-term storage.


## Notes

These sandwiches can be stored in the freezer for up to a year but are best consumed within a month.
