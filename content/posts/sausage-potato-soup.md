## Notes

**Pro Tips for the BEST Soup:** Cheddar Cheese: Extra-sharp is key. Shred your own for the smoothest melt. Sausage: Let it sear well before breaking apart for richer flavor. Potatoes: Dice evenly so every bite has the same perfect texture.
