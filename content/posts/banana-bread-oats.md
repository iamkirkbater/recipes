A delicious hot and gooey banana oatmeal dish with a touch of chocolate.

## Ingredients

- 50g oats
- 100g banana (ripe)
- 150g Greek yogurt (0%)
- 1 medium egg
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp brown sugar
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 10g chocolate pieces


## Instructions

1. Mix the oats, banana, yogurt, egg, cinnamon, sugar, and baking powder together.
2. Top with chocolate pieces.
3. Bake for 25-30 minutes at 200°C.


## Notes

For a weight loss-friendly option, you can omit the chocolate pieces on top.
