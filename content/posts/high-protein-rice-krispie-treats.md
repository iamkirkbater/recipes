A quick, easy, and delicious high-protein snack to satisfy sweet cravings while staying on track.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="30 minutes" Servings="6" >}}

## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp light butter
- 10 oz mini marshmallows
- 3 scoops vanilla whey protein
- 4 cups cereal of choice


## Instructions

1. Melt butter on low heat.
2. Add marshmallows and stir until smooth.
3. Gradually mix in protein powder, one scoop at a time.
4. Stir in your favorite cereal until evenly coated.
5. Press into a greased 9x9 dish, let cool for 30 minutes, then slice into 6 servings and enjoy.


## Notes

Perfect for meal prep or an on-the-go snack. Each serving contains 328 calories, 21g of protein, 47g of carbs, and 7g of fat.
