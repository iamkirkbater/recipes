A simple meal prep recipe featuring ground beef, shell pasta, and roasted garlic tomato sauce, perfect for making multiple servings.

{{< recipedetails Servings="5" >}}

## Ingredients

- 900g 96/4 lean ground beef - seasoned with salt and black pepper
- 1 box shell pasta
- 1.25 jars (4.5 cups) of Roasted Garlic tomato sauce - Classico
- Garlic Paste - optional
- Basil - optional
- Ricotta cheese
- Basil
- Red Pepper Flakes


## Instructions

1. Season the ground beef with salt and black pepper.
2. Cook the shell pasta according to package instructions.
3. In a pan, cook the seasoned ground beef until fully cooked through.
4. Add the roasted garlic tomato sauce to the cooked ground beef, mixing well. Optionally, add garlic paste and basil for additional flavor.
5. Combine the cooked pasta with the beef and sauce mixture, ensuring everything is evenly mixed.
6. Divide the meal prep equally into 5 portions. If you have a smaller appetite, you may divide into 6 portions.
7. Garnish each serving with ricotta cheese, basil, and red pepper flakes as desired.


## Notes

**Tips:** It's recommended not to over complicate the preparation. The large volume of shell pasta helps in making ample servings, making it suitable for meal prep.
