Delicious pizza burritos made in a slow cooker, packed with protein and flavor.

## Ingredients

- 4 pounds of chicken
- Basil
- Garlic powder
- Chili flakes
- Italian seasoning
- Onion powder
- 1 1⁄2 cups of pizza sauce
- Bone broth
- 70 grams 1⁄3 cream cheese
- 100 grams of turkey pepperoni
- 200 grams of light cheese
- Card-balanced tortilla
- Light provolone


## Instructions

1. Place the 4 pounds of chicken in the slow cooker.
2. Add seasonings: basil, garlic powder, chili flakes, Italian seasoning, onion powder, and some more garlic.
3. Pour 1 1⁄2 cups of pizza sauce over the top of the chicken.
4. Add bone broth and 70 grams of 1⁄3 cream cheese.
5. Cook on low in the slow cooker for 6 to 7 hours.
6. Once fully cooked, shred the chicken in the slow cooker.
7. Add 100 grams of turkey pepperoni and 200 grams of light cheese to the slow cooker.
8. Cover and cook for an additional 20 minutes until cheese is melted.
9. Fill card-balanced tortillas with the chicken mixture, adding light provolone if desired.
