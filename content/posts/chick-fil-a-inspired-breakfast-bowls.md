A high-protein, Chick-fil-A inspired breakfast bowl with hash browns, eggs, chicken, and cheese.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="30 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 85g shredded hash browns
- 50g whole egg (1 egg)
- 60g egg whites
- 113g lightly breaded chicken nuggets
- 28g fat free cheese
- Salt + pepper
- 15–30g pico


## Instructions

1. Mix all base ingredients in a 13 oz container.
2. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 min until eggs are cooked through.
3. Add cheese on top and heat again until melted.
4. Top with pico and enjoy.
