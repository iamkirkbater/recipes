Low-calorie cinnamon donuts that are perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth while staying on track with your diet.

## Ingredients

- 40g Oat Flour (blended oats)
- 1 Small Egg
- 100g Low Fat Greek Yoghurt (I used Vanilla YoPro @yoproau)
- 20g Brown Sugar Stevia
- 1 Tsp Cinnamon
- 2g Baking Powder
- Vanilla Extract
- 10g Cinnamon
- 10g Brown Sugar Stevia


## Instructions

1. In a bowl, add 40g of oat flour, 1 small egg, 100g of low fat Greek yogurt, 20g of brown sugar stevia, about a teaspoon of cinnamon, 2g of baking powder, a drop of vanilla extract and then give it a good mix until it's a nice thick consistency.
2. Grab a non-stick doughnut baking tray, place the mixture inside and bake it in the oven at 180 degrees Celsius for 15 minutes.
3. On a separate plate, add 10g of cinnamon and 10g of brown sugar stevia and mix together.
4. Dip each doughnut in the brown sugar stevia and cinnamon mixture. Enjoy and follow for more.


## Notes

Macros do not include the dip, which was 100g of low fat Greek yoghurt with 1 serving of chocolate fudge protein powder mixed together.
