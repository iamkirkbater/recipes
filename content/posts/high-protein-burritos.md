8 high protein burritos, perfect for meal prep with lean ground beef and a flavorful sauce.

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs lean ground beef (96/4)
- 1 packet taco seasoning
- 1/4 cup tomato paste
- 1/4 cup reduced-fat cream cheese
- 1/2 cup low-sodium beef broth
- 1/2 cup non-fat Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp light mayonnaise
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Pinch of salt
- 1/2 tbsp hot sauce
- 1 tsp lime juice
- Small amount of water to thin the sauce
- 8 wellness wraps


## Instructions

1. Chop up a white onion into the pan, let it cook until the onions are soft.
2. Add two pounds of extra lean ground beef to the pan.
3. Pour in 1/2 cup bone broth and add a packet of taco seasoning.
4. Mix in 1/4 cup tomato paste and 1/4 cup reduced-fat cream cheese.
5. Mix well and let it cook until the beef is fully cooked and ingredients are combined.
6. In a bowl, combine 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt with 1 tablespoon of light mayo.
7. Add 1/2 tablespoon of hot sauce, 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika, 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder, a pinch of salt, and 1 teaspoon lime juice.
8. Add a small amount of water to thin the sauce to a desired consistency.
9. Take 8 wellness wraps and distribute the beef filling evenly among them.
10. Roll up the burritos tightly.
11. Drizzle the prepared sauce over the top of the wrapped burritos.
