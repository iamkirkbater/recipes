A simple and nutritious pizza with Greek yogurt dough and high-protein toppings.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="25-30 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Plain Flour
- 1/3 cup Light Greek Yogurt
- 35g Pizza Sauce
- 25g Light Mozzarella Cheese
- 100g Raw Chicken Breast
- 35g Red Capsicum


## Instructions

1. Put in a bowl plain flour and Greek yogurt and mix until you form dough.
2. Put sauce, cheese, chicken, capsicum on top.
3. Throw into the oven at 180 degrees Celsius for 25-30 mins or until base is crispy.
4. Serve and enjoy.
