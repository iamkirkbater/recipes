A flavorful family-sized Mexican Chicken and Rice Bake enhanced with avocado and sour cream.

{{< recipedetails Servings="4" >}}

## Ingredients

- 160g uncooked rice
- 600g raw chicken
- 400g tomato salsa
- 2 tbsp garlic
- 1 large red onion, diced
- 220g black beans
- 220g corn kernels
- 20g fajita seasoning (or Mexican)
- 360ml chicken stock
- 80g Mexican cheese mix
- 200g avocado
- 100g low-fat sour cream


## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C fan forced (360°F).
2. Combine uncooked rice, raw chicken, tomato salsa, garlic, diced red onion, black beans, corn kernels, fajita seasoning, and chicken stock in a 3-liter bowl.
3. Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly combined.
4. Top with Mexican cheese mix.
5. Cover the top of the bowl with a metal tray or foil.
6. Bake for roughly 60 minutes. In the last 15 minutes, remove the cover to allow any moisture on top to evaporate.


## Notes

**Nutritional Information:** Calories for 1/4 servings without avocado & sour cream: 525 calories, 58.5g carbs, 11.5g fat, 48.1g protein. With avocado & sour cream: 661 calories, 71g carbs, 19.7g fat, 52.2g protein. If you want lower calories while including avocado/sour cream, consider portioning to 5 servings, with calories around 529.
