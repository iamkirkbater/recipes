Delicious high-protein mini tacos perfect for weight loss and muscle gain.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups shredded chicken
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
- ½ cup (56g) shredded cheese
- Seasonings: garlic powder, salt, pepper
- 1/2 cup greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp light mayo
- 1 tbsp adobo sauce
- Juice from 1/2 lime
- 1 tbsp honey
- Salt & pepper to taste
- 8 Mission Street Corn Tortillas (or Ole Wellness mini wraps)


## Instructions

1. Start by adding your shredded chicken into a bowl. Add fresh parsley, shredded cheddar cheese, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and juice from half a lime. Mix until well incorporated.
2. Add the chicken mixture onto your tortillas and place them in a well-lubricated medium-high heat pan. Cook until they're golden brown and crispy.
3. Combine greek yogurt, light mayo, adobo sauce, lime juice, honey, salt, and pepper in a blender. Blend until smooth.
