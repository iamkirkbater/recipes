A high-protein taco meal prep that makes 8 meals, each with 535 calories, perfect for meal prepping athletes.

## Ingredients

- 3 lbs 96/4 beef
- 1 packet taco seasoning, low sodium
- 300g rice
- 100g light sour cream
- 100g 1/3 fat cream cheese
- 1 bag Mexican 4 cheese, reduced fat
- 300g mild pico
- 1 cup corn
- 1 can black beans, no sodium


## Instructions

1. Put three pounds of beef in the pan.
2. Add one entire packet of taco seasoning. Mix this up and let it cook.
3. Cook 300g of rice with three cups of water.
4. Once the beef is done, add one cup of corn and one cup of black beans.
5. Add 300 grams of pico, 100 grams of cream cheese, and 100 grams of light sour cream.
6. Add 200 grams of taco sauce and your cooked rice.
7. Add one bag of Mexican four cheese. Mix this up so everything melts.
8. Divide the mixture into eight meal prep containers.
