A healthy and flavorful chicken fries recipe perfect for those looking to lose weight.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="30 minutes" Servings="2" >}}

## Ingredients

- 2 medium russet potatoes
- Avocado oil spray
- Salt, to taste
- 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
- Salt & pepper, to taste
- 1 tsp corn starch
- Avocado oil spray
- 1/4 cup light mayo
- 3 tbsp sweet chili sauce
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- Low cal yogurt ranch from Meal Prep Magic cookbook
- Green onions, for garnish


## Instructions

1. Cut the russet potatoes into thin french fry shapes.
2. Pat the fries dry and spray them with avocado oil. Season with salt.
3. Air fry the fries at 400 degrees until golden brown and crispy.
4. Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces.
5. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and corn starch. Mix until fully coated.
6. Air fry or bake the chicken as per the settings on screen.
7. Mix light mayo, sweet chili sauce, and soy sauce to make the bang bang sauce.
8. Toss the cooked chicken in the bang bang sauce.
9. Assemble the dish by adding fries, topped with chicken and drizzling yogurt ranch. Garnish with green onions.


## Notes

This recipe takes approximately 30 minutes.
