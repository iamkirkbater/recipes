A delicious and massive salad that's perfect for meal prep, high in protein and low in calories.

{{< recipedetails PrepTime="20 minutes" TotalTime="30 minutes" Servings="3" >}}

## Ingredients

- 170g Plain Non Fat Greek Yogurt (FAGE)
- 113g 2% Cottage Cheese (Good Culture)
- 40g Sriracha
- 30g Honey
- 5g Low Sodium Soy Sauce
- Juice and Zest from ½ Lime
- ½ tsp Sea Salt
- ¼ tsp Black Pepper
- ¼ tsp Garlic Powder
- ¼ tsp Smoked Paprika
- 300g Romaine
- 300g Lettuce
- ½ Bag Frozen Corn (8oz)
- 20oz Chicken Breast (weighed raw)
- Cajun Seasoning (for chicken breast)
- 100g Red Onion
- 200g Grape Tomatoes
- 40g Jalapeno
- 15g Cilantro
- 15g Minced Garlic
- 20g Parmesan (Cotija Cheese if you have it!)
- 1.5 tsp Sea Salt
- 1 tsp Black Pepper
- 1 tsp Garlic Powder
- 1 tsp Smoked Paprika


## Instructions

1. Add all your dressing ingredients into a small blender and blend till smooth. Add to fridge.
2. Preheat your stovetop pan to a 7/10 heat and add your frozen corn to it. Cook saute until they start to golden. Season with some sea salt and let the corn cool for 10 minutes.
3. Flatten and pat dry your chicken breasts, season each side with Cajun seasoning, and air fry on 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 10 minutes.
4. Put together the salad in a BIG bowl. Use only 1/3rd of the dressing in the salad. If meal prepping, keep the dressing on the side.
5. Serve and enjoy!
