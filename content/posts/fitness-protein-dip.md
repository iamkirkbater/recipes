A high-protein, low-calorie dip perfect for achieving fitness goals.

{{< recipedetails PrepTime="20 minutes" TotalTime="45 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup artichoke hearts, finely diced
- 1/2 cup frozen spinach, thawed, diced, and strained (or wilt 1-2 cups fresh spinach on stove)
- 1/4 cup blended lowfat cottage cheese
- 1/4 cup 0% Greek yogurt
- 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese
- 2 tablespoons lowfat mozzarella cheese
- Salt, to taste
- Pepper, to taste
- Garlic powder, to taste
- 2 tablespoons lowfat mozzarella cheese for topping


## Instructions

1. Finely dice 1/2 cup artichoke hearts and prepare 1/2 cup frozen spinach by thawing, dicing, and straining it. Alternatively, wilt 1-2 cups fresh spinach on the stove.
2. Mix the diced artichoke hearts and spinach in a baking-safe dish with 1/4 cup blended lowfat cottage cheese, 1/4 cup 0% Greek yogurt, 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese, 2 tablespoons lowfat mozzarella cheese, and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste.
3. Top the mixture with an additional 2 tablespoons of lowfat mozzarella cheese.
4. Bake at 350°F for 20-25 minutes until golden.
5. Serve the dip. Optionally, cut up and bake carb balance tortillas for macro-friendly chips.


## Notes

**Serving Tip:** This whole dish contains 28g of protein and 245 calories, making it excellent for any fitness goal. Consider cutting up and baking carb balance tortillas for a macro-friendly chip option.
