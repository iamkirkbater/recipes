A protein-packed five-layer dip perfect for quick and easy meal prep.

{{< recipedetails PrepTime="10 minutes" TotalTime="10 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup (130g) refried beans
- 1/3 cup (80g) Greek yogurt
- 1/3 cup (42g) fat-free cheese
- 4 oz (113g) extra lean ground beef
- 2 tbsp (30g) pico


## Instructions

1. In order, place refried beans, Greek yogurt, beef, and cheese into a container (use a 13 oz container if available).
2. Air fry at 375°F, bake at 400°F, or microwave until the cheese melts and everything warms up.
3. Enjoy!
