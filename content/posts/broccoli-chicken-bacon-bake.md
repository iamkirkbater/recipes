A high protein, low carb meal that's easy to prepare and perfect for meal prep.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="20 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- Boiled chicken breast (boiled in chicken broth & water) - 8 oz (about 1 large breast), seasoned with paprika, Tony’s, and adobo seasoning
- Steamed broccoli - 1.5 cups (6 oz)
- Light ranch dressing - 2 tbsp
- Bacon bits - 1 tbsp
- Mozzarella cheese - ¼ cup (1 oz)
- Parmesan cheese - 1 tbsp (½ oz)


## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
2. Boil the chicken breast in chicken broth and water, and season it with paprika, Tony’s, and adobo seasoning.
3. Steam the broccoli until tender.
4. In a baking dish, combine the boiled chicken, steamed broccoli, light ranch dressing, and bacon bits.
5. Sprinkle mozzarella and parmesan cheese on top of the mixture.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes or until done to your liking.
7. Serve and enjoy.
