A flavorful and smoky garlic parmesan ranch sauce, perfect for chicken fries, bowls, wraps, or any dish that needs a fire flavor.

{{< recipedetails Servings="2" >}}

## Ingredients

- ½ cup (113g) non-fat Greek yogurt
- ½ cup (113g) low-fat cottage cheese, blended smooth
- 1½ tbsp grated Parmesan
- 1 tbsp ranch seasoning
- ½ tsp smoked paprika
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- 1–2 tbsp water


## Instructions

1. Combine non-fat Greek yogurt and low-fat cottage cheese in a bowl. Blend until smooth.
2. Add grated Parmesan, ranch seasoning, smoked paprika, and garlic powder to the yogurt mixture.
3. Stir together until all ingredients are well mixed.
4. Thin the sauce with 1-2 tablespoons of water to reach your desired consistency.


## Notes

This smoky garlic parmesan ranch is ideal for adding flavor to chicken fries, bowls, wraps, or any other dish that needs a bit of fire.
