## Ingredients

- 1 cup of blended 2% cottage cheese @gayleafoodscoop
- 2 eggs
- 2/3 cup of all purpose flour
- 1 tsp of baking powder
- Seasonings: dried basil, dried oregano, dried parsley
- Pizza sauce
- 1 cup of Mozzarella cheese
- Pizza toppings: pepperoni, olives, onions etc


## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 F. 
2. To a large bowl add your blended cottage cheese and eggs. Whisk together. 
3. Add baking powder, flour, 1/4 cup of mozzarella cheese, and seasonings. Mix together. 
4. Spread mixture onto a lined baking tray to form pizza shapes. 
5. Please Note: you should get 8 mini pizzas from this recipe 
6. Top with pizza sauce, mozzarella, and your favourite pizza toppings. 
7. Bake for 25-30 minutes. 
8. Broil for 1-2 minutes to crisp the outside (optional).
