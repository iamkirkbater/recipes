Mini pizzas made with a cottage cheese base, topped with mozzarella and your favorite pizza toppings.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup of blended 2% cottage cheese
- 2 eggs
- 2/3 cup of all purpose flour
- 1 tsp of baking powder
- Seasonings: dried basil, dried oregano, dried parsley
- Pizza sauce
- 1 cup of Mozzarella cheese
- Pizza toppings: pepperoni, olives, onions etc


## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 F.
2. Blend the cottage cheese to make it smooth.
3. To a large bowl, add the blended cottage cheese and eggs. Whisk together.
4. Add baking powder, flour, 1/4 cup of mozzarella cheese, and seasonings. Mix together.
5. Spread mixture onto a lined baking tray to form pizza shapes.
6. Top with pizza sauce, mozzarella, and your favorite pizza toppings.
7. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
8. Broil for 1-2 minutes to crisp the outside (optional).


## Notes

**Tips:** You should get 8 mini pizzas from this recipe. You can make them as small or large as you'd like.
