Delicious Cajun chicken enchiladas with mozzarella cheese topping, perfect for a comforting meal.

{{< recipedetails PrepTime="10 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 630g chicken breast, diced
- 1 onion
- 1 red pepper
- 2 crushed garlic cloves
- 1 tsp oregano
- 2 tbsp cajun seasoning
- 300ml chicken stock
- 75g lightest cream cheese
- 4 large wraps
- 100g mozzarella cheese mix
- Chopped parsley for garnish


## Instructions

1. Add the onion and garlic to a pan with a spray of oil and cook for a minute or two to soften.
2. Add in the pepper and the chicken with the Cajun seasoning and oregano. Fry over a medium-high heat until cooked through.
3. Pour in the stock and the other tbsp Cajun and simmer for around 10 mins until it thickens, then reduce the heat a little and stir in the cream cheese and half of the mozzarella/cheddar mix.
4. Load up the wraps, place them into the airfryer and top with the remaining sauce from the pan and the remaining cheese.
5. Airfry on 190°C for around 10 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
6. Top with chopped parsley and some cajun dressing if you fancy (mix the remaining sauce with some natural yoghurt and a squeeze of lemon).
