A high-protein, flavorful street corn dip.

## Ingredients

- 4 oz chicken cooked, chopped chicken breast (I used blackened for extra flavor)
- 1 cup roasted corn
- ½ cup whipped cottage cheese (whip by blending in blender until smooth)
- ¼ cup crumbled feta
- ¼ cup finely chopped red onion
- juice of ½ lime
- 1 tbsp elote seasoning (I get mine from TJ’s)
- cilantro (for topping)


## Instructions

1. Whisk elote seasoning with whipped cottage cheese until combined.
2. Add all ingredients to a bowl and stir to combine. Enjoy!
