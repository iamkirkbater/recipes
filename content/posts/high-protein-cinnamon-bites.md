Delicious high-protein cinnamon bites, perfect for a healthy snack.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="20 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 120g white bread flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 180g 0% Greek yoghurt
- Cooking spray or egg wash to coat balls


## Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 190°C.
2. In a bowl, combine white bread flour, baking powder, and salt.
3. Add in the 0% Greek yoghurt and mix until a dough forms.
4. Divide the dough into 10 portions and shape each into a ball.
5. Coat each ball with cooking spray or egg wash.
6. Place the balls on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
7. Bake for 13-15 minutes if using a fan-assisted oven, or for 20 minutes without a fan.


## Notes

These high-protein cinnamon bites are rated 10/10 for their taste and are highly recommended for anyone looking for a delicious protein snack.
