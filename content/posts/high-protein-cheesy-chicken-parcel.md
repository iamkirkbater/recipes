A high-protein, cheesy chicken parcel perfect for lunch.

## Ingredients

- 90g cooked chicken (minced)
- 30g low-fat cheese
- 70g low-fat cottage cheese
- Light mayo
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic
- Parsley
- Warm tortilla


## Instructions

1. In a bowl, add cooked chicken (minced), low-fat cheese, low-fat cottage cheese, light mayo, salt, pepper, garlic, and chopped parsley.
2. Give it a mix until well combined.
3. On a warm tortilla, spread the mixture out in the center.
4. Tuck in the sides and fold it up into a parcel shape.
5. Toast the parcel in a pan until golden and crispy on both sides.
6. Enjoy your high-protein cheesy chicken parcel.


## Notes

This recipe packs over 50 grams of protein and is perfect for a quick lunch.
