A high-protein, cheesy dip with a blend of Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, and corn, perfect for game-day snacking.

## Ingredients

- 1½ cups low-fat Greek yogurt
- 1 cup low-fat whipped cottage cheese
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp onion powder
- 2 tsp taco seasoning
- ½ tsp salt
- 3 cups frozen corn, thawed & drained (I used Trader Joe’s Fire Roasted Corn)
- 1 bundle scallions sliced
- ½ jalapeño, deseeded & minced
- 3 tbsp chopped cilantro
- ½ cup @sargentocheese Cholula Shredded cheese
- ¾ cup Shredded cheddar


## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
2. Add the first 11 ingredients to an 8 by 8 baking dish and mix well.
3. Top with ¾ cup shredded cheddar and bake for 25 minutes.


## Notes

**Tip:** If you can't find whipped cottage cheese, add small curd cottage cheese to a blender and blend for 15 seconds.
