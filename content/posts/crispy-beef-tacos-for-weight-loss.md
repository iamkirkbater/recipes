A delicious and clean taco recipe that's both flavorful and low in calories.

{{< recipedetails PrepTime="10 minutes" TotalTime="20 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- Lean ground beef
- Finely chopped onion
- Garlic
- Oregano
- Onion powder
- Garlic powder
- Paprika
- Chili powder
- Cayenne pepper
- Sazon
- Starch
- Water
- High-fiber tortillas
- Reduced fat cheese
- Oil
- Low-fat Greek yogurt
- Red taco sauce
- Water
- Taco seasoning


## Instructions

1. Lightly oil your pan and heat it.
2. Once hot, add the lean ground beef and break it down with a wooden spoon.
3. Add finely chopped onion and garlic, letting everything cook together.
4. Mix in taco spices: oregano, onion powder, garlic powder, paprika, chili powder, cayenne pepper, and sazon.
5. Sprinkle in some starch and add water, mixing thoroughly. Cook until well combined.
6. Use high-fiber tortillas, laying ground beef on one side.
7. Sprinkle a little reduced fat cheese on each taco.
8. Oil a sheet tray and arrange the tacos on it.
9. Lightly spray tacos with oil and sprinkle with paprika.
10. Bake at 425°F (220°C) for about 10 minutes or until crispy.
11. Combine low-fat Greek yogurt, red taco sauce, water, and taco seasoning.
12. Mix well to create a high-protein dipping sauce.
13. Serve tacos with the dipping sauce and enjoy.
