A simple savoury snack with high protein and fiber.

## Ingredients

- 344g - canned chickpeas
- 1 tbsp - grated Parmesan cheese
- Paprika
- Cumin
- Salt & pepper
- Low cal Cooking spray


## Instructions

1. Drain and rinse chickpeas.
2. Pat dry with paper towel.
3. Spray cooking spray.
4. Add spices, cheese, salt and pepper.
5. Air fry - 390°F for 15 minutes.
6. Bake in oven - 375°F for 30 minutes, mix and then put back in for another 20 minutes. Enjoy!
