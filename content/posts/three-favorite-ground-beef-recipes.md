Three easy and nutrient-dense ground beef recipes to help you pack on muscle while eating clean.

## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of grass-fed ground beef
- 1 whole onion
- A boatload of garlic
- 1 cup of orzo
- 2 cups of beef bone broth
- As much spinach as you want
- As much cheese as you want
- Ground beef, seasoned
- Wedge potatoes
- Favorite burger toppings (e.g., onion, pickles, avocado)
- 2 pounds of ground beef
- 1 onion
- 1 package of coleslaw mix
- A little bit of water
- Soy sauce
- Chili paste
- A little bit of hoisin sauce


## Instructions

1. Put two pounds of grass-fed ground beef in a pan with a whole onion and a lot of garlic. Cook until done.
2. Add 1 cup of orzo and mix well. Add about 2 cups of beef bone broth.
3. Mix in as much spinach as you prefer and cook until orzo is done.
4. Add cheese to your liking and serve.
5. Cook ground beef thoroughly and season it as desired.
6. Cook wedge potatoes separately.
7. Place ground beef and potatoes together and top with burger toppings like onion, pickles, and avocado.
8. Cook two pounds of ground beef and an onion thoroughly.
9. Add one package of coleslaw mix and a little water to the pan. Mix well.
10. Stir in soy sauce, chili paste, and a little hoisin sauce for flavor. Mix thoroughly and serve.
