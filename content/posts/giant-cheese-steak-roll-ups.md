A healthy and protein-rich option for cheesy steak roll-ups with 400 calories, perfect for bulking or weight loss.

{{< recipedetails Servings="3" >}}

## Ingredients

- 1 lb stir fry steak
- 1 medium sweet onion
- 3 oz sharp cheddar (freshly shredded)
- 2 lavish breads from @josephspitabread
- Pickled jalapeños (optional)


## Instructions

1. Roll the cheesesteak mixture carefully like a giant wrap, ensuring the edges are sealed well.
2. Add a little cheese on the edge to help it stay intact.
3. Pan fry the roll-up until each side is nice and crispy.
