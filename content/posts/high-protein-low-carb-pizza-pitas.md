A versatile and delicious high protein, low carb meal perfect for busy people who love food.

## Ingredients

- Pita bread
- Pizza toppings of your choice (e.g., cheese, vegetables, meats)


## Instructions

1. Prepare your pita bread by adding your choice of pizza toppings.
2. Place in a preheated toaster or oven until the cheese is melted and the edges of the pita are crispy.
3. Serve immediately, paired with your sides of choice.


## Notes

**Customization Tips:** Feel free to customize your pizza pitas with your favorite toppings to suit your taste and dietary needs.
