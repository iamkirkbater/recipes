A tasty and healthy butter chicken recipe served with rice and a homemade naan bread.

{{< recipedetails Servings="2" >}}

## Ingredients

- 300g Chicken Breast
- 75g Greek Yogurt
- 1 tsp Minced Garlic
- 1 tsp Cumin
- 1/2 tsp Coriander
- 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
- 1/2 tsp Turmeric
- 1/2 tsp Ginger
- 50g Tomato Paste
- 80ml Light Cooking Cream
- 1 tsp Butter
- 1/2 cup Rice
- 80g Flour
- 80g Greek Yogurt
- Olive Oil


## Instructions

1. Dice the chicken breast and add to a bowl with the Greek yogurt and seasonings.
2. On a pan on medium heat, cook the chicken.
3. While the chicken is cooking, cook your rice your way, using a rice cooker if preferred.
4. Once the chicken is cooked through, mix the tomato paste with some water to make a sauce.
5. Add the tomato paste, cooking cream, and butter into the chicken and stir.
6. Plate with the rice to serve.
7. Mix the flour and Greek yogurt in a bowl to make a dough consistency.
8. Roll out to a Naan Bread shape.
9. Heat a pan on low-medium heat, with a splash of olive oil.
10. Flip a minute or two in, once one side is golden.
