A high-protein dessert that mimics the taste of Reese's.

{{< recipedetails PrepTime="5 minutes" TotalTime="20 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 60 g (⅓ cup) Greek yogurt
- 30 g (1 scoop) vanilla protein powder
- 32 g (¼ cup) peanut butter powder (PB2)
- 14 g (1 tbsp) milk chocolate chips (Lily’s or similar)


## Instructions

1. Mix yogurt, protein powder and peanut butter powder in a dish until smooth.
2. Melt chocolate chips in 30-second bursts until fully melted.
3. Spread chocolate over the top and freeze for 20 minutes.
4. Crack in and enjoy.


## Notes

**Macros (per bowl — 1 serving):** 342 calories, 19g carbs, 9g fat, 51g protein.
