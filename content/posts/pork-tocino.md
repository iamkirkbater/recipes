Thinly sliced sweet and sour pork 

{{< recipedetails PrepTime="30m" TotalTime="2-3 days" >}}

## Ingredients

- 1lb Pork Shoulder or Pork Belly
- 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
- 1/2 cup Pineapple Juice
- 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
- 1 tbsp Vinegar
- 4 cloves garlic
- 5-10 drops red food coloring
- Salt/Pepper to taste
- 1 cup rice
- 3 eggs


## Instructions

1. Thinly slice pork and combine all ingredients in a bag. Marinate for 2-3 days.

2. Cook pork on medium-low heat - too high and you burn the sugar and garlic
3. Serve on a bowl of rice with a sunny-side up egg
