A delicious high protein dip perfect for pairing with vegetables or chips.

{{< recipedetails Servings="4" >}}

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Bacon or turkey bacon
- 1/2 cup Green onions
- 2 tbsp Ranch seasoning
- 1 cup Cheddar cheese
- 2 cups Greek yogurt or dairy-free yogurt


## Instructions

1. Add half a cup of bacon, half a cup of green onion, two tablespoons of ranch seasoning, one cup of shredded cheddar cheese, and two cups of Greek yogurt into a bowl.
2. Mix until nice and well combined.


## Notes

This dip can be paired with any vegetable or chip of your liking.
