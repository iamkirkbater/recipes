A creamy one-pan dish combining Boursin cheese, sausage, and vegetables with orzo pasta.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="30 minutes" Servings="4" >}}

## Ingredients

- 1 lb chicken breast, thinly sliced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 4 caprese chicken sausage, diced
- 1 large zucchini, diced
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, cut in half
- 1 tsp SpiceX Daily Blend
- 1 tsp SpiceX Bella Blend
- 2 cups or 12 oz dry orzo
- 1 garlic & herb Boursin cheese
- 2.5 cups bone broth
- 1/2 cup water


## Instructions

1. Season thinly sliced chicken with SpiceX Daily Blend and set aside.
2. Dice zucchini, sausage, and cut cherry tomatoes in half and set aside.
3. In a large pan on high heat, add a drizzle of oil and sear the chicken breast for 4-5 minutes per side, then set aside and let it rest.
4. In the same pan, add the zucchini, sausage, and cherry tomatoes, cook for 3-4 minutes on high heat, and season with salt, pepper, garlic, and SpiceX Bella Blend, then set aside.
5. Add the orzo to the pan and toast for 1-2 minutes, then add the bone broth and half of the Boursin cheese.
6. Cover and let simmer for 10 minutes, then remove cover and add 1/2 cup of water. Add the remaining Boursin cheese and the sausage and vegetables, cook for another 5 minutes on medium-low heat. If too thick, adjust with water.
7. Cut up chicken and serve over the orzo. Garnish with chili flakes and enjoy!
