Delicious low-calorie jalapeño popper pockets with chipotle honey mustard sauce.

## Ingredients

- 170g Plain Non Fat Greek Yogurt (FAGE)
- 113g 2% Cottage Cheese (Good Culture)
- 63g Honey Mustard
- 22g Pickle Juice
- 30g Water
- 4 Chipotle Peppers in Adobo
- ½ tsp Sea Salt
- ¼ tsp Black Pepper
- ½ tsp Garlic Powder
- ½ tsp Onion Powder
- ½ tsp Smoked Paprika
- 16oz Chicken Tenders
- Cajun Seasoning
- 2 Jalapeños (roasted)
- 150g Plain Non Fat Greek Yogurt
- 3 Light Laughing Cow Cheese Wedges
- 1/4th Packet Ranch Dip Mix (about 8-10g)
- 42g Part Skim Mozzarella
- 60g Black Beans
- 60g Corn
- 60g Pico
- 8 Low Carb Tortillas
- 10g All Purpose Flour
- Cooking Spray


## Instructions

1. Add all your sauce ingredients into a small blender and blend until smooth. Store in the fridge.
2. Season the chicken tenders with Cajun seasoning and air fry at 400 degrees F for 9-10 minutes.
3. Roast the jalapeños in the air fryer at 400 degrees F for 5-7 minutes. Remove the stem and seeds and dice into small pieces.
4. Combine Greek yogurt, Laughing Cow cheese wedges, jalapeños, and ranch dip mix. Mix in mozzarella, chicken, black beans, corn, and pico.
5. Cut the tortilla in half, create pockets by folding, and use the filling to stuff the pockets. Use a paste made from flour and water as a sealant.
6. Spray the pockets with non-stick cooking spray and sprinkle with garlic powder. Air fry at 400 degrees F for 4-5 minutes until golden.
