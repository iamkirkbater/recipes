A simple crockpot chicken recipe with parmesan garlic sauce and pasta.

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs of chicken breast
- 2 cups of chicken broth
- 1 bottle of parmesan garlic sauce
- 1 lb box of pasta


## Instructions

1. Add 2 lbs of chicken breast to a crock pot with 2 cups of chicken broth and 1 bottle of parmesan garlic sauce.
2. Put the lid on the crock pot and cook on high for 4-6 hours until the chicken is fall apart tender.
3. Shred the chicken inside the crock pot.
4. Drop in a 1 lb box of pasta.
5. Cover the crock pot again and cook for another 30 minutes or until the pasta reaches the desired consistency.
6. Once the pasta is ready, the meal is ready to enjoy.
