High protein, low calorie, and low carb alternative to pizza. Each roll packs 23 grams of protein and only 4.4 grams of net carbs.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="Less than 15 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 1 low carb tortilla
- Freshly-shredded low fat mozzarella
- Refreshing tomato passata
- Beef
- Pepperoni slices


## Instructions

1. Use a low carb tortilla as the base.
2. Add freshly-shredded low fat mozzarella on top of the tortilla.
3. Spread refreshing tomato passata over the cheese.
4. Add beef and pepperoni slices on top.
5. Roll up the tortilla to form pizza rolls.
