## Ingredients

- 1 cup shredded hash browns
- 2 eggs
- Bacon bits or turkey sausage
- Sprinkle of cheese


## Instructions

1. Spray bakeable dish with cooking spray
2. Add 1 cup hashbrowns, salt and pepper to dish, spray top with cooking spray
3. Bake or Airfry at 400° for 15-20m
4. Make two "nests" in the hashbrowns, and crack eggs into them
5. Add bacon bits or turkey sausage
6. Add a sprinkle of cheese
7. Bake for 10-12m more
