Easy, affordable, and high-protein meal prep using crockpot for a flavorful chicken pasta.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="4 hours 45 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 2 pounds of chicken breast
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tbsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp thyme
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 2 cans cream of chicken, 50% sodium reduced
- 1 bag no yolk pasta
- 4 cups of chicken bone broth, protein edition
- 1 medium sized veggie bag


## Instructions

1. Place 2 pounds of chicken in the crockpot.
2. Add 2 cans of cream of chicken.
3. Season with 1 tbsp of salt, black pepper, thyme, and garlic powder.
4. Pour in 4 cups of chicken bone broth.
5. Set the crockpot to high and cook for 4 hours.
6. Break up the chicken after cooking.
7. Add 1 bag of no yolk pasta and 1 bag of veggies to the crockpot.
8. Mix everything together and cover.
9. Continue cooking for another 45 minutes.
10. Once done, divide the meal into 7 meal prep containers.


## Notes

The meal prep portions are based on macro-friendly specifications, containing approximately 440 calories, 43g protein, 6g fats, and 52g carbs per serving.
