A high-protein, lower-calorie pasta dish that's family friendly and makes great leftovers.

{{< recipedetails Servings="9" >}}

## Ingredients

- ground beef (I use 96/4) 9 ounces cooked
- jar of marinara sauce of choice
- Banza pasta, cooked


## Instructions

1. Combine the ground beef with a jar of marinara sauce of your choice.
2. Mix the cooked ground beef and marinara sauce with cooked Banza pasta in a big bowl.


## Notes

For serving, consider pairing with a big Italian side salad.

This dish makes about 9 servings and tastes good as leftovers.
