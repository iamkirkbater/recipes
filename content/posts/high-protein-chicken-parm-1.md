A low calorie, high protein chicken parmesan dish.

## Ingredients

- 350g - chicken breast
- 120g - low fat cottage cheese
- 20g - parmigiano reggiano
- 50ml - tomato sauce


## Instructions

1. Flatten chicken breasts and add some garlic + your choice of seasonings.
2. Bake at 425°F for 15 minutes.
3. While it's baking, blend your cottage cheese.
4. Top with tomato sauce, cottage cheese, and parmesan.
5. Broil for a few minutes or until cheese gets a bit brown.
