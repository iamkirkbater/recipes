High-protein chicken burritos with cottage cheese sauce and beef bacon.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="20 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 44 oz Chicken Breast
- Garlic Salt
- Onion Powder
- Smoked paprika
- 1/2 can of adobo peppers sauce
- 1.5 Cup Cottage Cheese (0% Fat)
- 3/4 Cup Enchilada Sauce
- 2 adobo peppers
- Two spoonfuls of the adobo pepper sauce
- 10 strips of beef bacon
- 2 cups Cup Fat Free Mozzarella
- 14 Low Calorie Tortillas (60 calorie extreme wellness)
- Light sour cream
- Light ranch mixed with wing sauce


## Instructions

1. Season the chicken breast with garlic salt, onion powder, and smoked paprika heavily for flavor.
2. Put the chicken into a bowl and add 1/2 can of adobo peppers sauce. Mix until the chicken is completely coated.
3. Add the chicken to your air fryer and cook at 375°F for 20 minutes.
4. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the sauce by blending cottage cheese, enchilada sauce, adobo peppers, and two spoonfuls of adobo pepper sauce until very smooth.
5. Cook 10 strips of beef bacon.
6. Chop the cooked chicken and bacon.
7. In a large bowl, combine chopped chicken, bacon, fat-free mozzarella, and the sauce mix.
8. Place the chicken mixture into each tortilla evenly.
9. Optional: Add a squirt of light sour cream to each burrito.
10. Optional: Serve with a mix of light ranch and wing sauce if desired.
