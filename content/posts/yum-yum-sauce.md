## Ingredients

- 1 cup Mayo
- 1/4 cup ketchup
- 1/4 stick melted butter
- 1-2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp Smoked paprika
- Salt to taste


## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients and store in the fridge
