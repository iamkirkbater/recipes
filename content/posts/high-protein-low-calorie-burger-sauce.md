A high protein, low calorie take on a signature burger sauce using Greek yogurt instead of mayonnaise.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="30 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 1 cup non fat Greek yogurt
- 1.5 tbsp tomato paste
- 1.5 tbsp yellow mustard
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp smoked chipotle powder
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp roasted garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp of salt, more to taste
- 1 tsp sugar substitute, I used @lakanto
- a splash of white vinegar
- 1 tsp avocado oil, @chosenfoods


## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
2. Adjust spices to your preference.
3. Thin the sauce with a little bit of water if needed.
4. Transfer the sauce to a deli container, cover, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.


## Notes

This sauce can be used to enhance the flavor of burgers, especially smash patties. If you find it too thick, adjust the consistency with water.
