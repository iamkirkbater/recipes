Easy and low-calorie sour gummy candy with a customizable flavor.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="4 hours" >}}

## Ingredients

- 200ml Boiling Water
- 1 Stick Zero Calorie Flavoured Drink Mix (your choice of flavour/brand)
- 14g Gelatine Powder (can be substituted for Agar Agar)
- 6g Citric Acid
- 30g Zero Calorie Sweetener Mixed With 8g Citric Acid


## Instructions

1. Place 200ml of boiling water into a cup or jar.
2. Add one stick of zero calorie flavoured drink mix, 14g of gelatine powder, and 6g of citric acid to the boiling water.
3. Mix well until everything is dissolved and evenly distribute across all gummy bear moulds. Alternatively, pour into a baking tray if you don't have moulds and cut into pieces once set.
4. Place into the fridge for a minimum of 4 hours or overnight until firm.
5. In a bowl, mix together 30g of zero calorie natural sweetener and 8g of citric acid for the sour coating.
6. Once gummies are firm, gently coat each candy in the sour coating.
7. Store leftovers in the fridge.


## Notes

**Customization:** You can repeat the preparation process with different flavors of zero calorie drink mix for a variety of gummy flavors.
