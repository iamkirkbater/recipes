Three high-protein sauces perfect for adding flavor to chicken, fries, bowls, wraps, or any dish.

{{< recipedetails Servings="2" >}}

## Ingredients

- ½ cup (113g) non-fat Greek yogurt
- ¼ cup (56g) low-fat cottage cheese, blended smooth
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp chili powder
- ½ tsp cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- Salt, to taste
- ½ cup (113g) non-fat Greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp (30g) light mayo
- 1 tbsp yellow mustard
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp sugar-free BBQ sauce
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- Salt, to taste
- ½ cup (113g) non-fat Greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp grated Parmesan
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 garlic clove (or ½ tsp garlic powder)
- Salt & black pepper, to taste


## Instructions

1. Combine Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, lime juice, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and salt in a bowl. If too thick, add a bit of water.
2. Mix Greek yogurt, light mayo, mustard, honey, BBQ sauce, lemon juice, and salt together in a bowl until smooth.
3. Blend Greek yogurt, Parmesan, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, salt, and black pepper in a bowl until well mixed.


## Notes

Each recipe makes about 2–4 servings. Double or triple as needed.
