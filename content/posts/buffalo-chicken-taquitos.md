Delicious and easy high-protein buffalo chicken taquitos

{{< recipedetails PrepTime="5 minutes" TotalTime="15 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 8 oz chicken
- 2 oz light cream cheese
- 2 oz taco cheese
- 1/4 cup buffalo sauce
- 100g plain Greek yogurt
- 3 tortillas


## Instructions

1. Mix everything into a bowl.
2. Roll up the mixture into tortillas.
3. Slice each rolled tortilla in half to make 6 equal parts.
4. Spray with oil.
5. Air fry at 375°F for 7 minutes and then at 400°F for 3 minutes.
6. If you’re oven baking, bake at 400°F for 7 minutes and then at 425°F for 3 minutes.


## Notes

**Macros:** Each taquito contains 232 calories, 21g protein, 9.5g fat, and 13g carbs.
