Macro-friendly Chick-Fil-Gay sandwich

## Ingredients

- 125g chicken breast
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Garlic powder
- Italian Bread crumbs
- Pickles
- 1 Low-calorie bun


## Instructions

1. Flatten chicken breast and season with Salt, Black Pepper, and Garlic Powder

2. Dredge chicken in bread crumbs and air fry 10-12m at 380 until 360f
3. Toast and assemble buns with Pickles and sauce of choice.
