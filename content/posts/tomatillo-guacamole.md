## Ingredients

- 2 avocados, chopped
- 1 cup chopped tomatillos
- 1 cup fresh spinach
- 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 1/4 cup cold water
- 2 Tbsp. fresh lime juice
- 1 jalapeño or serrano chile, chopped
- 1 scallion, chopped
- Salt to taste


## Instructions

1. Pulse avocados, tomatillos, spinach, cilantro, water, lime juice, jalapeño, and scallion in a food processor until slightly chunky; season with salt. (Guacamole may be made up to 2 days ahead — just stir well before serving.)
