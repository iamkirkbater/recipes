A healthier, high-protein alternative to the classic McChicken, using ground chicken pressed onto tortillas with a tangy sauce.

## Ingredients

- 500g ground chicken
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- 8 small flour tortillas (I used 4 medium)
- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
- 1 cup shredded iceberg lettuce
- 1/4 cup light mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup miracle whip (I didn't use this, still tasted insane)


## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix together the light mayonnaise and Miracle Whip until smooth. Set aside.
2. In a medium bowl, mix ground chicken with garlic powder, black pepper, and salt.
3. Press a thin layer of seasoned chicken onto the back of each flour tortilla, making sure it covers the surface evenly. Then sprinkle on the breadcrumbs for that added crunch factor.
4. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat with a little avocado oil. Place the tortilla chicken-side down and cook for 3-4 minutes until the chicken is golden and fully cooked. Flip and crisp up the tortilla side for another 2-3 minutes.
5. Spread a generous amount of McChicken sauce over the crispy chicken, top with shredded lettuce.
6. Fold it up like a taco, take a bite, and enjoy!


## Notes

**Macros:** 310 Calories per taco; 32g Protein; 30g Carbs; 8g Fat. If you eat two tacos, it's 620 Calories with 64g of protein.

This McChicken sauce recipe is the Canadian sauce recipe (sweeter), not the American one.
