550 Calories & 65g Protein
Taco Bell Beef Quesoritto

## Ingredients

- 24oz Lean Ground Beef (96/4)
- 1 Taco Seasoning Packet
- 1/4 Cup Red Enchilada Sauce
- 1 Cup Fat Free Cheddar Cheese
- 1/2 Cup White Rice (Dry)
- 1 Cup Chicken Bone Broth
- Cilantro
- 1/2 Lime (Squeezed)
- 8 Low Calorie Tortillas
- 8 Tbsps Nacho Cheese
- 1/2 Cup Non Fat Greek Yogurt


## Instructions

1. Brown ground beef with taco seasoning
2. Once beef is cooked add enchilada sauce and cheddar and mix until combined
3. Wash and cook rice with chicken broth, adding lime and cilantro after it comes off the heat
4. Assemble the quesarito by putting 2 tbsp of nacho cheese sauce on a tortilla, add another tortilla on top, and add 1/4 cooked rice and 1/4 beef mix. Drizzle with greek yogurt or sour cream and burrito roll.
