Delicious creamy chicken bacon ranch rolls, packed with 50g of protein each, perfect for meal prep.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="Not specified" >}}

## Ingredients

- 2.25KG Diced Chicken Breast (raw weight)
- 1 Tsp Olive Oil
- 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
- 1/2 Tsp Chilli Powder
- 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
- 1 Tsp Onion Powder
- 1 Tsp Salt
- 500g Low Fat Cottage Cheese
- 80g Softened Light Cream Cheese
- 2 - 4 Tsp Chipotle Paste
- 4 Diced Garlic Cloves
- 1 Tbsp Ranch Seasoning
- 1/2 Lime Juice
- 100ml Fat Free Milk
- 300g Low Fat Grated Cheese
- Chives
- 200g Cooked & Diced Bacon
- 15 Warm Tortillas


## Instructions

1. To your diced chicken breast add olive oil, then add the seasonings listed in the caption, mix well.
2. Cook the chicken on a pan on medium heat for 4 minutes on each side. Once cooked, set it aside.
3. In a blender cup, add low-fat cottage cheese, light cream cheese, diced garlic, chipotle paste, ranch seasoning, lime juice, and a bit of milk. Blend until creamy.
4. Add the creamy mixture to a bowl with the cooked chicken, some chives, diced bacon bits, and low-fat grated cheese. Mix well until combined.
5. Grab a warm tortilla, equally distribute the filling with some cilantro, roll tightly, and toast them.
6. Repeat the process for the other 14 rolls, wrap them up, and enjoy!


## Notes

**Reheat Instructions:** Take one roll out of the freezer, remove the foil, microwave for approximately 2 minutes, then air fry at 190C/375F for 5 - 8 minutes or until hot & crispy. This last step can also be done in a pan.
