## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
- 2 1/2 Tbsp. honey
- 3 Tbsp. chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
- 1/2 Tbsp. salt
- 4 Tbsp. water
- 1/2 cup neutral oil (I used avocado oil)
- 1/2 tsp. ground black pepper
- 1/4 tsp. dried oregano
- ---
- 2 bellpeppers sliced
- 1 red onion sliced
- 2 tsp oregano
- 1 tsp salt


## Instructions

1. Make the [Chipotle Steak](/g/home/r/chipotle-steak-marinade)
2. Combine all honey vinaigrette ingredients
3. Cook the peppers and onions with oregano until soft
4. Wrap the quesadilla like a burrito and then pan fry on both sides
5. Mix a bit of sour cream into the vinaigrette and cut the burritos on a diagonal and turn them into quesadillas.
