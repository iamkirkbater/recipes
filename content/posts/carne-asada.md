Carne Asada Protein

## Ingredients

- 2lbs Skirt Steak (Flank Steak)
- 1 Orange (peeled, whole)
- 4tbsp Soy Sauce
- Juice of 1 lime
- 8 cloves garlic
- 2tbsp Cumin
- 1tbsp kosher salt
- 1tbsp black pepper
- 1tbsp Onion Powder
- 3 dried ancho chilies
- 2 dried guajillo chilies
- 1/3 of a 7.5oz can of chipotles in adobo
- 20g honey
- 1/3 bushel of cilantro (incluing stems)
- 1.5tbsp avocado oil


## Instructions

1. Prep the Marinade: De-stem and de-seed dried chilies and microwave for ~45-60 seconds to toast (they will rehydrate as they sit in the marindate)
2. Add all the marinade ingredients to a blender and blend until completely smooth (~2min)
3. Add marinade to Zip-loc bag(s) and add steak. Close bags and marinate for 8-12 hours.
4. ---
5. Preheat grill to max heat for at least 15 minutes
6. Remove steak from marinade and put on grill.
7. Flip every minute for 5-6 minutes.
8. Remove, let rest for 10 minutes. Slice against the grain.
