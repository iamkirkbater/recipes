Southwest Mac Salad

## Ingredients

- 8 oz macaroni noodles (227g)
- ¾ cup finely diced red bell pepper about ½ pepper (100g)
- ⅓ cup thinly sliced celery about 1-2 stalks (55g)
- ⅓ cup finely diced red onion (45g)
- 2 large hard boiled eggs, finely diced
- ---
- ¾ cup mayonnaise I use olive oil mayo (175g)
- ¼ cup sour cream (70g)
- 1 Tablespoon granulated sugar
- 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1.5 tbsp Southwest Seasoning


## Instructions

1. 

2. Prepare macaroni noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse immediately with cold water, then drizzle with a teaspoon of olive oil and gently stir so the noodles don't stick together as they sit.  
3. In a large bowl, combine macaroni noodles and other ingredients. Stir until well-combined. Set aside and prepare your dressing.  
4. To prepare your macaroni salad dressing, combine all dressing ingredients 
5. Pour dressing over your macaroni salad ingredients and toss until well-combined. 
6. For best flavor, cover bowl and allow macaroni salad to chill for at least one hour. Stir once more before serving and enjoy!

If preparing in advance, reserve 1/2 of dressing and set aside and mix into bowl immediately before serving.
