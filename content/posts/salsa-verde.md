## Ingredients

- 2 Avacados
- 2 Jalapenos, without stems
- 1 Garlic Clove
- 6 Tomatillos
- 1/4 White onion
- Juice of 4 Limes
- Salt to taste
- 2 cups water
- 1/4 Bunch Cilantro, roughly chopped


## Instructions

1. Put all items except for Cilantro into blender and blend.
Halfway through blending, add Cilantro
