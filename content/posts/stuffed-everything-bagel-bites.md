Healthy high-protein stuffed bagel bites made with Greek yogurt and almond flour, filled with cream cheese, and seasoned with Everything Bagel seasoning.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 cup almond flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tbsp Everything Bagel seasoning
- 1/2 block cream cheese


## Instructions

1. Roll your cream cheese into little balls and freeze until firm.
2. Mix together the yogurt, almond flour, baking powder, and Everything Bagel seasoning to form a dough. Knead for a few minutes.
3. Form the dough around each frozen cream cheese ball. Spray with oil.
4. Air fry at 360°F for 10 minutes until golden and crispy. Take them out & enjoy!
