A high-protein, no-bake cheesecake that mimics the taste of traditional cheesecake but is lower in calories and higher in protein.

{{< recipedetails PrepTime="5 minutes" TotalTime="5 minutes" Servings="2" >}}

## Ingredients

- 1 cup fat-free Greek yogurt (220g)
- 1/4 cup whipped cream cheese (40g)
- 1 scoop vanilla whey protein (30g)
- 1/4 cup @pbfit powdered peanut butter (30g)
- 1 tbsp cheesecake pudding mix (8g)
- 1 tbsp zero calorie powdered sweetener
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt
- 1/2 crushed graham cracker sheet (8g)
- 2 tbsp whipped cream (5g)
- 2 tsp melted peanut butter (10g)


## Instructions

1. Add Greek yogurt to a bowl with whipped cream cheese, vanilla whey protein, PB Fit powdered peanut butter, sweetener, cheesecake pudding mix, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix until thick.
2. Transfer the mixture to a serving bowl, place it in the freezer for a short time, then top with crushed graham crackers, whipped cream, and melted peanut butter.


## Notes

**Macros:** Each serving contains 310 calories, 11g fat, 21g carbs, and 36g protein.
