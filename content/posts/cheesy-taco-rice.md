A protein-packed, low-calorie cheesy taco rice dish, cooked in one pan.

{{< recipedetails Servings="3" >}}

## Ingredients

- 500g lean beef mince
- Taco seasoning
- 750ml beef stock
- 300g white rice
- 30g tomato puree
- 70g low-fat cheddar


## Instructions

1. Add 500g of lean beef mince to a pan.
2. Add taco seasoning and fry off the beef mince.
3. Then add 750ml of beef stock, 300g of white rice, and 30g of tomato puree.
4. Add the lid and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
5. If desired, add 70g low-fat cheddar and let it melt.
