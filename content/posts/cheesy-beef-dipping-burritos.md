Delicious low-calorie, high-protein burritos perfect for dipping.

## Ingredients

- 16oz 96/4 Lean Ground Beef
- 1 Packet Taco Seasoning
- 56g 2% Mexican Cheese
- 2 Light Laughing Cow Cheese Wedges
- 6g Nutritional Yeast
- 120g Reduced Sodium Chicken Broth
- 8 Light Mozzarella Sticks
- 8 Low Carb Tortillas
- 170g Plain Non Fat Greek Yogurt
- 113g 2% Cottage Cheese
- 113g Taco Bell Fire Sauce
- 8g Taco Seasoning


## Instructions

1. Blend 170g Plain Non Fat Greek Yogurt, 113g 2% Cottage Cheese, 113g Taco Bell Fire Sauce, and 8g Taco Seasoning until smooth. Refrigerate to cool and thicken.
2. Cook 16 ounces of 96/4 Lean Ground Beef on high heat until browned. Add a packet of taco seasoning and 90g of chicken broth to keep it moist.
3. Lower the heat and add Mexican cheese, cheese wedges, nutritional yeast, and 30g more chicken broth. Mix until smooth.
4. Freeze mozzarella sticks, then cut them in half long ways after freezing.
5. Lay down a low carb tortilla, place 1/8th of the filling in the middle, and place a mozzarella stick on each side of the filling. Fold in the sides of the tortilla, then fold the bottom and top and press tightly.
6. Repeat the folding process for all 8 burritos.
7. Spray the burritos with non-stick cooking spray and place them in the air fryer. Air fry at 400°F for 4 minutes, flipping halfway through.
