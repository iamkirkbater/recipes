High-protein sausage pizza pockets using a tortilla base, filled with cheese and ground turkey, and seasoned with herbs.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="10 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- 1 mission carb balance tortilla
- 56g fat free mozzarella cheese
- 1/4 cup pizza sauce
- 2oz Ground turkey
- basil
- oregano
- garlic powder
- red pepper flakes


## Instructions

1. Spread pizza sauce on one tortilla and slice in half.
2. Fold one half as shown.
3. Add cheese, ground turkey (or meat filling of choice).
4. Seal open side with a fork.
5. Spray avocado oil on top and add herbs.
6. Air fry on 400F for 10 minutes.
7. Demolish 😭


## Notes

If you prefer a sweeter sauce taste, use marinara instead of pizza sauce.
