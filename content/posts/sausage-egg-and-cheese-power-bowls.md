A high-protein, low-carb breakfast idea featuring eggs, chicken sausage, and cheese.

{{< recipedetails PrepTime="10 minutes" TotalTime="45 minutes" Servings="6" >}}

## Ingredients

- 6 eggs
- 3 cups egg whites
- 3 cups cottage cheese
- 3 cups mixed fajita onions (frozen)
- 6 organic chicken sausage links
- 6 servings of fat-free cheese, mozzarella or cheddar


## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Crack one egg into each container (6 containers).
3. Pour half a cup of egg whites into each container.
4. Add half a cup of cottage cheese into each container.
5. Add half a cup of mixed fajita onions into each container.
6. Add one sausage link to each container.
7. Add a serving of fat-free cheese to each container.
8. Give the ingredients in each container a stir to combine.
9. Place the containers in the oven and bake for 35 minutes.
10. Once out of the oven, let the bowls sit for 10 minutes before serving.
