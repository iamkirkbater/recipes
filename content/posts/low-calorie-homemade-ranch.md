A lighter ranch dressing made with Greek yogurt, buttermilk, and fresh herbs, offering the same great flavor with fewer calories.

{{< recipedetails TotalTime="30 minutes" >}}

## Ingredients

- ½ cup low-fat plain Greek yogurt
- ¼ cup buttermilk
- ¼ cup light sour cream
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp onion powder
- ½ tsp salt or MSG
- 2 tsp chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tsp chopped fresh dill
- 1 tsp chopped fresh chives
- 1-2 tsp fresh lemon juice


## Instructions

1. Whisk together the Greek yogurt, buttermilk, and sour cream in a bowl until smooth.
2. Mix in the garlic powder, onion powder, and salt.
3. Fold in the fresh herbs—parsley, dill, and chives.
4. Stir in the lemon juice, starting with 1 teaspoon, and adjust to taste.
5. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to let the flavors blend.


## Notes

**Pro Tip:** Still better than blue cheese.
