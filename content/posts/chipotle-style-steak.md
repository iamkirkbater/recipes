## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 lb of top round or eye round steak
- 3 tbsp chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 cup neutral oil


## Instructions

1. Blend all ingredients, marinate as long as possible.

Cook steaks on medium-high heat.
