## Ingredients

- 4.5 oz. Chicken
- 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
- 1/2 tsp Salt
- 1/2 tsp Pepper
- 1/2 tsp Chipotle Powder
- 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
- --------
- Toppings:
- 2 slices sourdough rounds
- 1/2 avocado
- Provolone (light optional)
- Spinach
- 2 slices center cut bacon
- 1-2 tbsp of Protein Chipotle Sauce
- ---------
- Protein chipotle Sauce:
- Cilantro
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 Chipotle in Adobos
- 50g light mayo
- 150g non fat greek yogurt


## Instructions

1. Season chicken breasts with seasoning
2. Cook chicken over medium high heat, let rest 5m
3. Cut avocado, assemble sandwiches on toasted sourdough rounds
